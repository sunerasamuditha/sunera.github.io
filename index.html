About Me

Hi there! My name is Sunera Samuditha, and I am a passionate computer engineering undergraduate who thrives on building innovative and impactful projects. My interest in technology and problem-solving sparked early, and I've been channeling it into creating smarter, more efficient systems ever since.

Currently, I’m diving deep into web development as part of my 1st-year coursework at KDU, while also managing exciting projects like LEARNY, an online learning platform my team and I are developing. Working on this project has enhanced my skills in database management, PHP, and Bootstrap-based frontend design.

One of my proudest achievements is being part of the DrySmart+ Rain Safe Drying System project, where my team and I developed a smart, weather-safe clothes drying solution using IoT technology. This system integrates real-time weather monitoring, rain detection, and automated clothes movement mechanisms to ensure efficiency and energy conservation.

Beyond academics, I’ve had the privilege of contributing to STEM Education projects like Ganitha Saviya, impacting over 80,000 students through seminars and evaluations across 25 districts. I’m also shaping future leaders by teaching leadership to inspire the younger generation and actively participating in the IEEE student chapter at KDU.
